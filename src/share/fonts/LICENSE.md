# Inter font

Bundled fonts:
- `Inter-Regular.ttf`
- `Inter-Bold.ttf`

Source: https://github.com/rsms/inter (v4.0 release).

Licensed under the SIL Open Font License 1.1 — full text at
<https://github.com/rsms/inter/blob/v4.0/LICENSE.txt>.

To swap in a different font: drop your TTF/OTF files into this directory,
update the imports in `src/fonts.ts`, and redeploy. Satori accepts TTF and
OTF — not WOFF2 — so convert if needed.
